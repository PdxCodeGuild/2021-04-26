The uniqueness of the i3

The bmw I3 is a b segment high roof hatchback manufactured and marketed by BMW with a traditional single motor rear wheel drive electric motor and a two speed transmission. This was BMW's first ever zero emissions vehicle producted for mass market outside of concept vehicles like the MINI cooper E that would later come into production in late 2020.

The journey of the i3:
  1. Due to how niche the i3 is in the market, all i3s can be made in a single plant! all are manufactured in the Leipzig plant.
  2. After that they are shipped by dealer order to the destination country via a large cargo boat, protected by large amounts of wrap and degradable coverings
  3. for i3s like mine, they are often picked up in places like california and hauled via truck to the selling dealer.
  4. the final destination is the first (or second) owner to take it home and use.
