One of the most influentual progressive rock bands of the 1970's

Yes was formed in 1968 in London by singer Jon Anderson, bassist Chris Squire, guitarist Peter Banks, keyboardist Tony Kaye, and drummer Bill Bruford. Through the years, the band has gone through numerous changes to include 19 different musicians. The one constant was founding member Chris Squire until his death in 2015. Without a question, the early 1970's marked the high point of their musical progressive rock production.

Two of my favorite Yes albums are Close to the Edge. and Relayer.

Artwork by Roger Dean.

How the band was named: Anderson suggested that they call the new band Life. Squire suggested that it be called World. Banks responded, simply, "yes", and that was how the band were named.
