Batman - The Dark Knight

Summary

Batman is a fictional character appearing in comic books published by DC comics. The character was created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger and first appeared in Detective Comics #27(May 1939). The character is referred by titles such as Caped Crusader,The Dark Knightand The World's Greatest Detective.

Batman's secret identity is Bruce Wayne, a billionaire, playboy, philanthropist and the owner of Wayne Enterprises. After witnessing the gruesome murder of his parents as a child, he swore to take revenge on criminals and bring justice. Wayne trains himself to achieve perfection and fights crime. Batman fights crime in the fictional city of Gotham with the assistance of his butler Alfred Pennyworth and other vigilantes such as Batgirl, Robin, Nightwingand the police commissioner Jim Gordon.
